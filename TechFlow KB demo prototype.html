Frequently Asked Questions

Accounts

How do I reset my password?
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Check your email and follow the reset steps. Ensure your new password meets security requirements.
Why am I locked out of my account?
After 3 failed login attempts, your account automatically locks for 15 minutes for security. Wait or contact support for immediate assistance.
How to update my profile details?
Go to Account Settings → Edit Profile. You can update your name, contact information, and preferences there.
Can I change my registered email?
Only administrators can update login emails through the CRM Admin Console. Contact your system administrator for email changes.

CRM Features

Why can't I add a new contact?
Check your permission level. Clear your browser cache or try refreshing the page. Ensure you have the correct user role for contact management.
How to export customer data?
Navigate to Reports → Export → Select CSV format. Choose the data fields you want to export and download the file.
How to assign leads to a sales rep?
Go to Leads → Select the specific lead → Click "Assign to User" and choose the appropriate sales representative.

Billing

How do I update my payment method?
Go to Billing → Payment Settings → Update card details. Ensure your card information is current and valid.
Where can I view invoices?
Access Billing → Invoices section to view and download PDF copies of all your invoices.
What should I do if my payment fails?
Verify your card's validity and available funds. If issues persist, contact our finance team at finance@techflow.com for assistance.

Technical Setup

Why is the CRM not loading?
Check your internet connection and try using supported browsers like Chrome, Edge, or Firefox (latest two versions).
Which browsers are supported?
Chrome, Edge, and Firefox (latest two versions). Ensure you're using an updated browser for optimal performance.
How do I enable browser cookies?
Go to Browser Settings → Privacy & Security → Enable cookies. This is required for proper system functionality.
How to clear cache/cookies?
In Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data. Select "Cookies and site data" and "Cached images and files."

Error Codes

Error 401 – Unauthorized
Log out and log back in. Your authentication token may have expired. Ensure you have proper permissions.
Error 500 – Server Error
Try again in 5 minutes. If persistent, report the issue to support as it indicates a server-side problem.
Error 404 – Page Not Found
The link may be broken. Check the URL for accuracy or navigate from the main dashboard.
Error 7001 – API Timeout
Retry the connection. This may indicate network latency or temporary service interruption.

Email & Notifications

Why am I not receiving notifications?
Check your Email Preferences settings and spam folder. Ensure notifications are enabled for your account.
How do I disable daily summary emails?
Go to Settings → Notifications → Toggle off "Daily Summary" to stop receiving daily email summaries.

Reset Your Corporate Network Password (Self-Service)

Before You Start

  • Ensure you have access to your registered email
  • Have your employee ID ready (if applicable)
  • Use a private, secure network

Step 1: Access the Password Reset Portal

Navigate to the Self-Service Password Management Portal at [Link to TechFlow Password Portal]

Tip: Save this link for future use.

Step 2: Create a New Password

Enter your old password if applicable (when you just want to change your current one)

Step 3: Password Requirements

Password must meet these criteria:
  • At least 12 characters long
  • One lowercase character
  • One uppercase character
  • One number, symbol, or whitespace character
  • Do not reuse your last 5 passwords

Step 4: Confirm and Log In

Enter your new password again to confirm. Click 'Continue'. Wait 1 minute for the change to propagate across the network. Log in to your computer and applications using your new password.

Troubleshooting & Help

Still locked out? Create an 'Urgent Password Reset' ticket by emailing support@techflow.com from a personal device, and an agent will contact you within 15 minutes.

Failed Payment or Payment Difficulty

Before You Start

  • Verify you have Super Admin or "Manage payments and subscriptions" permissions
  • Have your payment method details ready
  • Ensure you're using the correct billing account

Step 1: Verify Admin/Permission Status

Confirm you have Super Admin or Manage payments and subscriptions permissions in your TechFlow account, as only these users can update payment details.

Step 2: Navigate to Billing Settings

Click your account name in the top right, then select Account & Billing. Go to the Payment Methods tab.

Step 3: Review and Update the Card

Check the current payment method for any typos in the card number, expiration date, or billing address. If you are certain the card details are correct, delete and re-add the card to force a fresh authorization with the payment processor.

Step 4: Contact Your Bank (External Step)

Call your bank or credit card company to confirm if they are blocking a charge from "TechFlow" and authorize the transaction.

Contact TechFlow Billing

Action: If the card is confirmed as active and authorized by your bank, email the TechFlow Billing Team at billing@techflow.com for further assistance with the payment attempt.

Connect Personal Email (Gmail / Outlook) to Send & Log Emails

Note: You can connect your personal inbox to TechFlow without being an admin — unless your company restricts mailbox connections.

Step 1: Access Settings

In your TechFlow account, click the settings icon in the top navigation bar.

Step 2: Navigate to General Settings

Navigate to the left sidebar menu, click General.

Step 3: Click the Email Tab

Click the Email tab in the settings menu.

Step 4: Connect Personal Email

Scroll down to the email section and click Connect personal email.

Step 5: Connect Your Inbox

Click Connect your inbox.

Step 6: Enter Email Address

In the text field, enter your email address.

Step 7: Provider Connection

TechFlow will attempt to recommend which email provider you should connect with. Click Connect to [your email account], then continue the steps below for your specific provider.

Step 8: Microsoft Outlook Setup

  1. Read through what to expect when you connect your Office 365 inbox to TechFlow, then click Continue.
  2. You'll be prompted to log in to your Office 365 account. Enter the email address of the Office 365 account you want to use.
  3. You'll be redirected back to your settings where the connection will be verified and completed.

Pro Tips

  • Install the add-in for Outlook (Windows/PC) to use TechFlow in your Outlook
  • Install the extension for Google Chrome to use TechFlow in your Gmail
